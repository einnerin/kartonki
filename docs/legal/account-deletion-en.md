# Account Deletion — Kartonki

**Effective date:** 2026-05-03
**Contact:** einerin40@gmail.com

This document explains how to delete your account and associated data in Kartonki.

## What gets deleted

When you request deletion:
- ✅ Your Firebase Authentication account (email / Google link)
- ✅ Cloud profile: display name, email, profile photo
- ✅ Online PvP statistics (wins / losses / draws)
- ✅ Active matchmaking queue slots

## What does **not** get deleted

- ❌ Local learning progress on your device (cards, decks, streaks). This data belongs to the device, not the account, and is removed only when you uninstall the app.
- ❌ Finished PvP match records you participated in. These are shared with your opponents; one-sided deletion would break their history. After your account is deleted, only your uid and your at-the-time display name remain in those records; no other personal data.
- ❌ Anonymous behavioral analytics in Firebase Analytics. It expires automatically after 14 months and is not tied to the deleted account (it uses a separate Installation ID that resets when the app is reinstalled).

## How to delete — two ways

### Method 1: in-app (recommended)

1. Open Kartonki on your device
2. Sign in to the Google account you want to delete (if not already signed in)
3. Menu → **Settings**
4. **Account** section → tap **Delete account**
5. Confirm in the dialog

Deletion happens immediately. If the app asks you to sign in again (Firebase requires a recent login for deletion as a security measure), sign in once more — deletion will continue automatically.

### Method 2: email request (if you don't have access to the app)

Send an email to **einerin40@gmail.com** with the following:

- Subject: `Kartonki — account deletion request`
- The email of the Google account to delete
- (Optional) your in-app display name — to make it easier to find

Deletion is performed within **30 days** of receiving your request. A confirmation email will be sent to the same address once done.

## Timeline

| Method | Time |
|---|---|
| In-app | Immediate |
| Email | Up to 30 days |

If something's unclear or doesn't work — write to **einerin40@gmail.com**.
